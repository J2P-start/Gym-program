# Gym Program

A personal gym workout plan to track and follow scheduled training sessions.

## Overview

This program outlines a structured workout routine to follow at the gym. It serves as a personal reference for exercises, sets, reps, and rest days to keep training consistent and organized.

## What's Included

- Workout schedule (which muscle groups to train each day)
- Exercises per session with sets and reps
- Rest day recommendations
- Progressive overload guidance

## How to Use

1. Check the schedule for today's workout
2. Follow the listed exercises in order
3. Log your sets and reps to track progress
4. Rest on designated rest days to allow recovery

## Goals

- Build strength and muscle consistently
- Stay on track with a structured plan
- Progress over time through gradual increases in weight or reps
